# SpendLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Data Collection
SpendLens collects NO personal data. 

## What we store
The only data stored is your extension settings:
- Selected AI model preference
- Theme preference (light/dark/auto)
- Custom sites you add manually
- Whether the extension is enabled or disabled

This data is stored locally in your browser using 
chrome.storage.sync and never leaves your device.

## What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your prompts
- We do not collect browsing history
- We do not send any data to external servers
- We do not use analytics or tracking

## Third party services
SpendLens does not communicate with any external servers.
All token counting and cost estimation happens locally 
in your browser.
